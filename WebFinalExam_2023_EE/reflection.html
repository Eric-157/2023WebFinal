Eric's Web Final

Never Before

Before CPWD, I never attempted drawing or making games. Ever since joining, I've had multiple chances to learn art, make games, and pursue these new passions I've discovered. And senior year I got to culminate all that I've learned through a game jam.

Completed

There are two things I am happy about completing. The first is my personal PC. I built it during COVID, and it's been chugging along since. The second is the game jam. Having a project I can say I worked on for a purpose besides just learning is nice. It was fun and I hope to participate in more jams in the future.

Achievement

Being valedictorian I guess was my biggest achievement. I did not try for it, I did not put in effort for it, and I didn't really want to be 1st, but the school would say otherwise and in fact are saying otherwise and I have to play along with the lie. The closest thing to an achievement for me is being able to stand up and give a speech in front of the school. Twice.
